What is this?

You don't want to reveal your salary expectation to the company you're applying to. The company doesn't want to reveal their budget.

But neither of you want to waste time if your expectations are not compatible.

This app lets you know if your salary expectation is compatible with the company's budget, without revealing any numbers to either parties.

The code for this project is available at salary-standoff-ui and salary-standoff-api.

How to use this?

First, you insert your minimum acceptable salary. This is the value below which you think it would not be worth to take the job.

The previous step will return you a link that you can send to the recruiter. Don't forget to tell the recruiter the details about the number you inserted in the form!

With the link, the recruiter can now check if your salary expectation is compatible with the company budget.

The recruiter can then tell you if you're compatible or not.

Note that during the process you never reveal you salary expectation to the recruiter nor does the recruiter reveal to you the company budget!
